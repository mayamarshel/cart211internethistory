ART IN CONTEXT

As the internet and connectivity expand we are exposed to limitless amounts of art from all corners of the world. This allows for unprecidented creativity and conversation but not without a cost. Art, especially visual, is always created in a certain context. Context could include place, time period, political enviornment, and more all forming the mindset of that artist when they created the piece. Here I try and identify art further than just the title and date, connecting some of my favorite pieces to their original contexts. Click the image text to begin.
